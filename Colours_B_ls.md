### Colours - Language structures

***Question:*** **'What colour is it?' (De que cor é?)**  
***Answer:*** **'It's ..*colour*.. .' (É ..*cor*.. .)**

***Question:*** **'What colour is this/that?' (De que cor é isto/isso?)**  
***Answer:*** **'It's/that's ..*colour*.. .' (É/Isso é ..*cor*.. .)**
